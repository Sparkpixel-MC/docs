# Minecraft Basics Guide

Minecraft is a sandbox building game where players can freely explore, build, and survive in a world made of blocks.

## Game Modes

### Survival Mode
- Need to gather resources
- Craft tools and weapons
- Fight against monsters
- Maintain health and hunger

### Creative Mode
- Unlimited resources
- Can fly
- Focus on building
- Cannot take damage

## Survival Tips

1. **First Day**
   - Gather wood to make tools
   - Find coal to make torches
   - Build shelter before nightfall

2. **Food Acquisition**
   - Kill animals for meat
   - Grow wheat
   - Fish

3. **Resource Gathering**
   - Mine ores
   - Explore caves
   - Collect rare items

## Advanced Tips

- Use enchanting tables to enhance equipment
- Brew potions to gain abilities
- Explore the Nether and the End
- Trade with villagers

## Conclusion

Minecraft is a game full of possibilities. Whether you like building, exploring, or adventuring, you'll find your own fun.
